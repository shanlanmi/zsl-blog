### Cell

key|function
:-:|-
⇧⏎|Add a new cell
⌥⌘⏎|Split Cell
⇧⌘X|Cut Cell
⇧⌘C|Copy Cell
⇧⌘V|Paste Cell
⇧⌘K|Delete Cell
⇧⌘I|Insert a new cell
⌥⌘1|Convert to text cell
⌥⌘2|Convert to code cell
⌥⌘3|Convert to markdown cell
⌥⌘4|Convert to LaTe cell
⌥⌘5|Convert to diagram cell
⌥⌘↑|Move Cell Up
⌥⌘↓|Move Cell Down
